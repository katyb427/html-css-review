Old-Fashioned Apple Pie

Yield: 2 pies

Ingredients

  • 2 pie pastries
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 teaspoons flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 6 to 8 large tart apple
  • 2 tablespoons butter, or margarine

Directions

  1. Line 9-inch pie pan, using half the pastry
  2. Roll out remaining pastry for top crust
  3. Set oven for hot, 400 degrees F
  4. Mix sugar, flour, and spices
  5. Spread a little of the sugar mixture over the bottom of pastry lined pan
  6. Pare apples; cut in quarters
  7. Remove cores; slice thin
  8. Arrange apples in pan; sprinkle with remaining sugar mixture
  9. Dot with butter or margarine
  10. Cut slits in top crust; adjust over apples
  11. Seal edges; trim; flute
  12. Bake 50 to 60 minutes until apples are tender
